WELCOME TO GULU UNIVERSITY LIBRARY

Gulu University (GU) Library congratulates you upon being offered a higher education opportunity at Gulu University. At this stage of your life’s journey, you are considered adult enough to take decisions that may shape your future. However, it is still important that you are further supported in your academic endeavors for a better future.

It is therefore important for you to, at the very start, understand the structure of the GU’s Library and Information Services (LIS). LIS offers a number of services that are directed towards improving your academic experience while at the University and after the University life.
To start with, LIS department’s mandate is to plan, organize, direct, control, coordinate and manage all University Library and Information services. This is emphasized in Statute 85 of 2005, which requires the department to put in place all the necessary Information and Communication Technologies aimed at easing stakeholders’ access to information.
